You cannot apply to remove this overseas entity

We cannot remove an overseas entity if it:

  • owns any property or land in the UK
  • still appears on any land registry as the registered owner of property or land in the UK

If you've already registered the disposal of property or land with the relevant land registry, you need to wait until it has been removed from their records.

You can search the land registries online to check if the overseas entity is still listed on their records (they will charge a fee):

  • HM Land Registry (for England and Wales)
  • Land Register of Scotland
  • Land Registry for Northern Ireland

You may also need to file your overseas entity update statement if it is due.

Contact Companies House if you need to speak to someone.
