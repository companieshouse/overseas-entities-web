What having personal information protected means

Some people can apply to protect personal information from being shown on the Companies House public register if they meet certain criteria. This process includes:

  • providing evidence that they are eligible to have their details protected
  • paying a £100 fee

You should only select ‘yes’ if any of the beneficial owners or managing officers have already done this.
