Help with the Overseas Entity ID

This is given to an overseas entity when it registers with Companies House. You can find it:

  • by searching for the entity on the Companies House register (opens in a new tab)
  • in the Notice of Registration email that was sent to the overseas entity
  • in the acceptance email that was sent to the person who registered the overseas entity
