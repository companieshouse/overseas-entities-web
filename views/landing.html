Register an overseas entity and tell us about its beneficial owners

Use this service to register an overseas entity's beneficial owner, so that they can buy or sell land in the UK.

! Warning You can be fined £500 a day and have difficulty buying or selling land if you do not register.

You'll need:

  • to sign in to or create a Companies House account
  • a credit or debit card to pay the £100 fee

You'll need to tell us the overseas entity's:

  • contact details
  • registered office address
  • legal authority and legal form
  • country of registration and registration number

You'll need to tell us the beneficial owner's:

  • full name
  • date of birth
  • correspondence address and home address
  • nationality
  • nature of control
  • verification status

Registering takes around 10 minutes.
